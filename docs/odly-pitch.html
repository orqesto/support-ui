Core Platform

Everything your support team needs

Built for multi-channel teams that need speed, structure, and intelligence — without stitching together a dozen tools.

🤖
AI-Powered Triage
Every message is automatically classified: spam filtered, priority set, category assigned, lead flagged, and ticket-worthiness determined — before a human ever touches it.
📥
Unified Inbox
All inbound communication from every channel lands in one place. Filter by channel, department, priority, or status. No context switching.
🎫
Ticket Management
Convert messages to structured tickets with SLA timers, priority levels, assignment, internal notes, file attachments, and full lifecycle tracking.
💡
AI Suggested Answers
When an agent opens a message, Odly searches the knowledge base and surfaces a ready-to-use reply with sources and a confidence score.
⚡
Rules & Auto-Assignment
Build if-then rules to automatically assign messages to agents or departments, set priorities, trigger replies, and route work — no code required.
📊
Analytics & SLA
Track response times, breach rates, agent performance, and channel volume. SLA targets enforced per channel with live compliance dashboards.
📚
Knowledge Base
Searchable library of answers, docs, and FAQs. Resolve a message and save the reply to the KB in one click. Past client conversations can be marked as KB items — your support history becomes institutional knowledge.
👤
Contact Management
Every sender builds a profile automatically — full message history, ticket history, and context across channels, always at your agent's fingertips.

AI Features

What the AI does for your team

Odly's AI pipeline runs on every inbound message. Each module can be toggled on or off per organization.

🔑
Bring Your Own AI — no lock-in
Connect your existing OpenAI, Anthropic, Qwen, or any compatible provider. Privacy-sensitive? Run a local model entirely on your own infrastructure. You own the keys, the data, and the cost.
Module What it does Value
Spam Filter Auto Removes newsletters, out-of-office replies, phishing, and marketing noise Cleaner inbox, zero agent time on junk
Categorization AI Assigns each message to a support category (Billing, Technical, Sales…) Instant routing, no manual tagging
Priority Detection AI Scores urgency: Low → Medium → High → Critical Critical issues surface immediately
Lead Detection AI Flags inbound sales leads before they get lost in support queues Revenue pipeline, not just support
Suggested Answer AI Pulls from your KB and surfaces a ready draft reply with sources Faster resolution, consistent tone
Auto-Reply Auto Sends configurable automatic responses for common request types 24/7 response without extra headcount
Translation AI Translates any message or ticket to 19+ languages inline Global support with local teams
How it works
01
Message arrives
Email, Telegram, Slack, or chat widget message is received and ingested into the unified inbox.
02
AI pipeline runs
Spam check, categorization, priority scoring, lead detection, and embedding — all in seconds.
03
Rules fire
Automation rules assign the message, set priority, trigger notifications, or route to the right department.
04
Agent responds
Agent opens message, sees AI-suggested answer with KB sources, edits if needed, and replies in one click.
05
Ticket if needed
Complex cases become tickets with SLA timers, internal notes, attachments, and Jira sync.
06
Track & improve
Analytics dashboard shows response times, SLA compliance, agent performance, and AI model usage.
